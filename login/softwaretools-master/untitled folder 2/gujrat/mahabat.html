Mahabat Maqbara Palace

Mahabat Maqbara Palace, also Mausoleum of Bahaduddinbhai Hasainbhai, is a mausoleum in Junagadh, India, that was once home to the Muslims rulers the Nawabs of Junagadh. Tomb of Mahabat Khan The city of Junagadh has other 18th-century mausoleums built by the nawabs in the area around Chitkana Chowk.
