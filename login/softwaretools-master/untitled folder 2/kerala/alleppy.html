Alleppey, alias Alappuzha is a city situated on the Laccadive Sea in the southern part of India in the state of Kerala. A lleppey is one of the favourite and frequently visited tourist destinations in Kerala. The place is famous for its houseboat cruises, beautiful beaches, canals, lagoons, and the famous annual event Punnamada Lake’s snake boat race. Alleppey is blessed with amazing flora and fauna, coconut, palms, green paddy fields, backwaters and more. If we look back to the 3rd Century BC – 4th Century AD, Alleppey was a cultural de stination in Kerala since then. It has a rich culture and heritage.
