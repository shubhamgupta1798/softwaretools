NAME: UmamaheswaraSwamytemple

Shri Yaganti Uma Maheswara temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva, and this is one of the famous temples of Lord Shiva in India. Every year Maha Shivaratri is celebrated and a large number of devotees come from all parts of Andhra Prad esh. The temple was constructed and completed by 1st Vijayanagara Sangama king - Harihara Bukkarayalu in 15th Century.
