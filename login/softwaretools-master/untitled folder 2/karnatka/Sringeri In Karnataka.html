NAME: sringeri in karnataka

Sringeri / ಶೃಂಗೇರಿ also written as Shringeri is a hill town and Taluk headquarters located in Chik kamagaluru district in the state of Karnataka, It is the site of the first maṭha (Sringeri Sharada Peeta) established by Adi Shankara, Hindu theologian and exponent of the Advaita Vedanta philosophy, in the 8th century CE It is located on the banks of the river Tungā and also a historical Temple (1200 years).
