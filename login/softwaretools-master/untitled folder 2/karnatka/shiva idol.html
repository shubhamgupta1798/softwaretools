Murudeshwar is a small town in the Bhatkal Taluk of Uttara Kannada district in the state of Karnataka, India. Murudeshwar derives its name from the other name of the Hindu god Shiva. The town is famous for the world's second-tallest Shiva statue. Th e town lies on the coast of the Arabian Sea and is also well-known for the Murudeshwar Temple.
