Mathura

Mathura is a sacred city in Uttar Pradesh, northern India. 
The deity Lord Krishna is said to have been born on the 
site of Sri Krishna Janma Bhoomi, a Hindu temple. Dotting 
the Yamuna River are 25 ghats (flights of steps down to 
the water), of which Vishram Ghat is considered the holiest. 
Sati Burj is a 16th-century memorial tower. Dwarkadhish 
Temple has a carved entrance and a black-marble idol of 
Lord Krishna.
