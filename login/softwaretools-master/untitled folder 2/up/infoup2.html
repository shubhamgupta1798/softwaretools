Fatehpur Sikri

Fatehpur Sikri is a small city in northern India, just west
 of Agra, founded by a 16th-century Mughal emperor. Red 
 sandstone buildings cluster at its center. Buland Darwaza 
 gate is the entrance to Jama Masjid mosque. Nearby is the 
 marble Tomb of Salim Chishti. Diwan-E-Khas hall has a 
 carved central pillar. Jodha Bais Palace is a mix of Hindu 
 and Mughal styles, next to the 5-story Panch Mahal that 
 overlooks the site.
