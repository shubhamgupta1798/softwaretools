The Basilica of Bom Jesus

The Basilica of Bom Jesus or Borea Jezuchi Bajilika 
is located in Goa, India,and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
 The basilica holds the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier.
Address: Old Goa Rd, Bainguinim, Goa 403402
Opened: May 15, 1605
Architectural style: Baroque architecture

HOW TO REACH ?


Nearest Airport: Goa International Airport
